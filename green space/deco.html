Decorative Plants

  • Fern plant

    Description: Ferns The lushness of ferns makes them ideal for those looking to spruce up a dull corner or make a big visual impact without too much effort. The fronds of the fern are fresh and vibrant, and often grow over and above the pot, which makes them look verdant and beautiful. It has air cleansing properties as well, needs indirect sunlight and regular watering.

  • Aralia Plant

    Description:Aralia, or spikenard, is a genus of the family Araliaceae, consisting of 68 accepted species of deciduous or evergreen trees, shrubs, and rhizomatous herbaceous perennials.A low-maintenance houseplant, aralias require little specialized care and fare best in warm, humid rooms. Aralias are prone to infestation by various insects, so inspect them regularly for signs of bugs.
